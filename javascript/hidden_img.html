What's making them die?

Poaching is what's killing all the tigers in Asia. That's really bad. Tiger fur, teeth, and even whiskers are sold on the Black Market at massive prices. As a result of these habits, the tiger population continues to drop like a fly. Many programs like the Wold Wildlife Fund are stepping up to protect this endangered species. Although it won't save the population entirely, a step is a step.
