Antelope Island: It's The Place to Be!

Why should you go?

Antelope Island is one of the most popular state parks in Utah. it is the largest island in the Great Salt Lake and is home to a variety of animals like the American Bison, antelopes, coyotes, and waterfowl. The sceneary is absolutely BREATHTAKING. There are many things you can do like hiking, sightseeing, and just enjoying nature. The ranch, AKA the Fielding Garr Ranch, is a historic site is located near Garr Springs, which was often used as a water source for the indigenous tribes in the area. As you hike up the rocks and hills, you wil feel like you're on top of the world.

Four Reasons Why

  • Its country trails are amazing for hiking and moutian biking.
  • You can get very close encounters with the wild bison.
  • When you hike up to Buffalo Point, you can see the entire Salt Lake Valley.
  • This state park welcomes dogs, so make sure to bring along your furry friend!
  • You can support Utah by enjoying beautiful the scenary!
