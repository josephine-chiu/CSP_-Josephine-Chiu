Why should you go?

Antelope Island is one of the most popular state parks in Utah. it is the largest island in the Great Salt Lake and is home to a variety of animals like the American Bison, antelopes, coyotes, and waterfowl. The sceneary is absolutely BREATHTAKING. As you hike up the rocks and hills, you wil feel like you're on top of the world.

Four Reasons Why

  • Its country trails are amazing for hiking and moutian biking.
  • You can get very close encounters with the wild bison.
  • When you hike up to Buffalo Point, you can see the entire Salt Lake Valley.
  • This state park welcomes dogs, so make sure to bring along your furry friend!

The Burning Bush - Hans Zimmer

The Burning Bush is from the movie The Prince of Egypt and it completely destroyed me when I first to it. It still does to this day. It's so emotional that it might as well knock your socks off. Hans Zimmer masterfully constructs a calm setting with tranquil strings playing in the background. However, as the situation intensifies, so does the music. Such an awe-invoking piece that will forever be remembered.
