Taylor Swift: The Most Underated Song

Soon You'll Get Better ft. The Chicks

This song has a very special place in my heart. It's also not very known to many Taylor Swift fans for reasons I don't know. It was released in 2019 in the Lover album. Taylor has never performed this song live for a particular reason: it talks about her mom getting cancer. Since this is such a sensitive topic, she decided that this song not be performed live.

Fun Facts!

  • This song is about her mother's struggle with cancer.
  • The Chicks are a country band originated from Dallas, TX
  • The track peaked at number 63 on the US Billboard Hot 100
  • On April 18, 2020, Taylor performed this song as part of the One World: Together at Home.
  • The album, Lover was an album that acted like a letter to love which invoked all the feelings that come with love.
